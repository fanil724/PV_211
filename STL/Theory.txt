STL - Standard Template Library

1. Sequence containers:		array, vector, deque, forward_list, list;
2. Associative containers:	map, set, multimap, multiset;
3. Adaptor containers:		stack, queue - очередь, priority_queue - очередь с приоритетами;
4. Unordered associative containers: unordered_map, unordered_set, unordered_multimap, unodered_multiset;
									 unordered - несортированный.
